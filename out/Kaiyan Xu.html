• Some concepts overlap between Customer Profile and Value Map. Example: if a product solves pains by saving time, it creates the gain of efficiency at the same time - customers' pain usually come from their difficulty in completing their jobs.
  • Keurig was designed for the "job" of making coffee quickly and conveniently without the lengths of traditional brewings. Pains come from the time, efforts, and the cleanup afterwards, required by brewing traditionally. Gains are by Keurig providing convenience and speed.
